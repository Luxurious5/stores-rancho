The Ultimate Guide to Choosing an Upholstered Swivel Chair for Your Home

Kareen Hadded December 7, 2024

When it comes to furnishing your home, comfort, style, and versatility are key factors to consider. An upholstered swivel chair is one piece of furniture that checks all these boxes. Whether you're looking to add a cozy reading nook, a chic accent chair, or a functional office chair, an upholstered swivel chair offers both practicality and aesthetic appeal. This guide will walk you through the benefits of swivel chairs, tips for choosing the right one, and how to incorporate them into your home design.

What is an Upholstered Swivel Chair?

An upholstered swivel chair is a type of chair that features cushioned fabric or leather seating and can rotate 360 degrees. This functional design allows for easy movement, providing flexibility and comfort, making it a great addition to various settings in your home. Upholstered swivel chairs come in different styles, sizes, and fabrics, offering options to suit both modern and traditional interiors.

Why Choose an Upholstered Swivel Chair?

Upholstered swivel chairs have become increasingly popular due to their unique combination of comfort, convenience, and style. Here are some reasons why they might be the perfect fit for your home:

  1. Comfortable Seating: The cushioning and upholstery provide a soft, supportive seat for long periods of sitting, making these chairs ideal for reading, watching TV, or relaxing.

  2. 360-Degree Mobility: The swivel function allows you to move effortlessly without having to get up. This is especially useful in spaces where you need to turn to face different directions, such as in living rooms or home offices.

  3. Space-Saving Design: Swivel chairs are compact and easy to move, making them a great option for smaller spaces. Their ability to rotate means you can easily access various areas of the room without needing extra floor space.

  4. Versatility: These chairs work well in different rooms of your home. Whether placed in a living room, bedroom, home office, or even a nursery, upholstered swivel chairs blend with various decor styles.

  5. Stylish Appeal: Upholstered swivel chairs come in a variety of fabric options and designs, from elegant velvet to casual linen, making them a chic and functional addition to your decor.

Choosing the Right Upholstered Swivel Chair

Selecting the perfect upholstered swivel chair for your home depends on several factors, including the room’s decor, your personal style, and the chair’s function. Here are some tips to help you make the best choice:

1. Consider the Room and Purpose

Start by thinking about where you plan to place the chair and its intended use. If you need a comfortable spot for reading or lounging, opt for a plush swivel chair with a deep seat and supportive backrest. For a home office, choose a swivel chair with adjustable features for better ergonomics.

2. Choose the Right Upholstery Material

Upholstery material is crucial not only for the look and feel of the chair but also for its durability. Common upholstery options include:

  • Velvet: Velvet swivel chairs are luxurious and sophisticated, perfect for adding a touch of elegance to your living room or bedroom.
  • Leather: Leather swivel chairs offer a polished, professional look, making them ideal for home offices or modern spaces.
  • Linen or Cotton: These fabrics are breathable and available in a wide range of colors and patterns, making them versatile for casual settings or eclectic designs.
  • Faux Leather: If you prefer a more affordable and easy-to-maintain option, faux leather offers the look of leather without the high cost.

3. Size and Scale

Make sure the size of the swivel chair fits comfortably in the space. Consider the dimensions of the chair and how it will complement the other furniture in the room. If you have a smaller space, look for chairs with a compact design that don’t overwhelm the room.

4. Frame and Construction

The frame of the swivel chair is important for ensuring durability. Look for chairs with sturdy, well-constructed frames made from materials like hardwood or metal. A strong base will help the chair last longer and support the swivel mechanism.

5. Style and Design

Upholstered swivel chairs come in various styles, from mid-century modern to contemporary, traditional, and even bohemian. Choose a design that complements your existing furniture and matches the aesthetic of your room. Key design elements to consider include armrest shape, backrest height, and base style.

How to Style an Upholstered Swivel Chair in Your Home

Upholstered swivel chairs are versatile enough to work in a variety of settings. Here are some styling ideas for different rooms in your home:

1. Living Room

In the living room, coaster swivel chair make great accent pieces. Pair them with a sleek sofa to create a cozy conversation area. Choose a swivel chair with a modern design to complement your contemporary living room or a more traditional swivel chair to blend with classic furniture.

  • Tip: Add throw pillows in complementary colors and textures to make the swivel chair more inviting.

2. Home Office

For your home office, an upholstered swivel chair provides comfort during long hours of work. Choose a design with ergonomic features, such as adjustable height, lumbar support, and smooth-rolling casters, to improve posture and productivity. Leather or faux leather chairs work well in professional settings.

  • Tip: Keep the chair in neutral tones like gray, black, or beige to maintain a sleek, professional look.

3. Bedroom

In the bedroom, an upholstered swivel chair can serve as a cozy reading nook or a place to relax. Opt for soft fabrics like linen or cotton and place the chair next to a window or in a corner with a small side table for added function.

  • Tip: Add a soft throw blanket and a floor lamp to create a comfortable and stylish space for reading.

4. Nursery or Kids’ Room

An upholstered swivel chair can also be a great addition to a nursery or kids' room. Choose a chair with durable, easy-to-clean upholstery like cotton or microfiber, and place it near the crib for comfort during late-night feedings.

  • Tip: Look for swivel chairs with a smooth, quiet mechanism to avoid disturbing the baby.

Maintaining Your Upholstered Swivel Chair

To ensure your coaster swivel chair remains in great condition, regular maintenance is essential. Here are a few tips for keeping your chair looking its best:

  • Vacuum Regularly: Dust and debris can accumulate on your upholstery. Use a handheld vacuum or upholstery brush to remove dirt from fabric surfaces.
  • Clean Spills Immediately: If you spill something on your chair, blot it gently with a clean cloth to prevent staining. For fabric chairs, use a fabric cleaner; for leather, use a leather cleaner and conditioner.
  • Rotate Cushions: If your swivel chair has removable cushions, rotate them periodically to ensure even wear.
  • Check the Swivel Mechanism: Over time, the swivel mechanism can become loose. Periodically check the base and tighten any screws if needed.

Conclusion

An upholstered swivel chair is a stylish and functional piece of furniture that can enhance any room in your home. Whether you're looking for a cozy reading spot, a comfortable office chair, or a chic accent piece, there’s a swivel chair that fits your needs. By considering factors like upholstery material, size, and design, you can find the perfect chair to suit your space and style. With its combination of comfort and versatility, an upholstered swivel chair is sure to become a beloved part of your home decor.
